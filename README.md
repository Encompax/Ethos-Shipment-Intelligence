# Ethos-Shipping-Intelligence
End to End ERP ecosystem within the Encompax family under governance authority carried out by Encompax Core through the Governance charter. 

- Connects and collaborates with the other ecosystems within the Encompax federation with the purpose and intent of complete control, tracking, monitoring and shipping products witin a manufacturing or distribution workflow environment.

- Gains Data Insight for decision making through Marengo Data Insights.

- Ensures Quality standards through collaboration with Kardia Quality Workflow System.
